Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Moltbot" mean?

It's a play on "Molt" (shedding/transformation) + "bot". The mascot is a space lobster ü¶û (crustaceans molt as they grow).

Is Moltbot free?

The software is free and open source. You pay for AI API usage ($50-100/month) and hosting ($5-20/month).

Do I need to know how to code?

Not if you use a done-for-you service like OwnAgent.ai. DIY setup requires basic command-line skills.

Can I use Moltbot for my business?

Yes! Thousands of businesses use it for operations, support, marketing, and more.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is reactive (you go to it). Moltbot is proactive (it comes to you). Plus persistent memory, tool integration, and 24/7 automation.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Moltbot runs on YOUR infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control. GDPR/HIPAA compliant if configured properly.

Which AI models does it support?

Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 (OpenAI), DeepSeek, Gemini, and local models via Ollama.

Can I integrate with my existing tools?

Yes! Gmail, Calendar, Slack, JIRA, Notion, and any tool with an API. Check ClawdHub for pre-built integrations.
